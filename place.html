Maldives Beach

The Maldives boast white, fine-grained sand that hugs the tropical flora. The stunning natural beauty of these islands draws visitors from all over the world. Beaches are situated among coral islands and provide you with relationship goals to cross off your list.

Palawan, Philippines

For good reason, Palawan, one of the top destinations to visit in the Philippines, is frequently regarded as one of the best islands in the world. It is known for its white sand beaches and islets, as well as its crystal blue seas and diverse marine life and shipwrecks.

Hawaii Beach

Hawaii is known for its beaches, some of which are filled with white sand.
